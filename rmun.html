RUIAMUN 2025 invites delegates to navigate this complex landscape, addressing challenges and opportunities in global governance structures that must adapt to this new reality.

About RUIAMUN 2025

What is RUIAMUN?

RUIAMUN is Ramnarain Ruia College's flagship Model United Nations conference, bringing together students from across the country to engage in diplomatic negotiations, critical thinking, and public speaking. Now in its 4th year, RUIAMUN has established itself as one of Mumbai's premier collegiate MUN conferences.

Message from the Secretary-General

Khushi Chaturvedi

Secretary-General, RUIAMUN 2025

Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors,

It is with immense pride and enthusiasm that I invite you to the 4th edition of RUIAMUN. As we navigate an increasingly complex global landscape, our conference theme 'Reshaping Global Governance: Adapting to a Multipolar World' aims to engage delegates in discussions that mirror the challenges faced by international organizations today.

RUIAMUN has grown from strength to strength over the past years, establishing itself as a platform for substantive debate, diplomatic negotiation, and collaborative problem-solving. This year, we are raising the bar even higher with enhanced committee experiences, distinguished guest speakers, and interactive social events.

I encourage you to approach each committee session with curiosity, critical thinking, and the courage to propose innovative solutions. Remember that the skills you develop here—research, public speaking, negotiation, and consensus-building—will serve you well beyond this conference.

On behalf of the entire Secretariat, I look forward to welcoming you to what promises to be an unforgettable RUIAMUN 2025!

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Ramnarain Ruia College, established in 1937, is a premier institution of higher education in Mumbai. With a rich legacy spanning over eight decades, we continue to be at the forefront of academic excellence and innovation.

Our institution prides itself on maintaining high academic standards while fostering a vibrant environment for holistic development of students.

Student Leadership

Ruia Student Council

The Voice of Students

The Ruia Student Council (RSC) is the official student representative body of Ramnarain Ruia College. We act as a bridge between the students and administration, ensuring that student concerns are addressed and their voices are heard.

Our mission is to enrich campus life by organizing events, facilitating clubs, and creating opportunities for student growth and leadership development.

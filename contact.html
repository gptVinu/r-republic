How to Reach Us

By Train

Matunga Station (Central Line) is a 5-minute walk from the college. King's Circle Station (Harbour Line) is a 10-minute walk away.

By Bus

Multiple BEST bus routes stop at Matunga East Bus Stand, just 2 minutes from the college entrance.

By Car

Limited parking is available near the college. We recommend using public transportation when possible.
